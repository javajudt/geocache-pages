With the relatively unbelievably nice weather we've been having this December, I wanted to get out to place some caches. I didn't have a good name picked out for some of them, so this one celebrates the winter solstice!

The winter solstice in the northern hemisphere occurs when the sun is directly overhead the Tropic of Capricorn, the Sun's southern-most position relative to Earth. It goes without saying that the southern hemisphere experiences the summer solstice at this time. The winter solstice typically occurs on December 20th or 21st; however, it does rarely fall on the 20th or the 23rd.

Nothing too special about the cache. Spirit Mound is nearby, so go check that out if you haven't yet!

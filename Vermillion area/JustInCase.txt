<!-- Cache info -->
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #0468e1;">This cache is located along Vermillion's walking/biking path that runs parallel to Stanford Street and Highway 50. The whole trail is extra-wide and paved.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #023a7e;">If you are driving, please do not park on the street. Park at one of the nearby businesses or in the little pulloff area by the utility boxes, and make the short walk over.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #0468e1;">The seasoned cacher will probably have no trouble with finding it. May be more challenging for others.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #023a7e;">Enjoy the find, and enjoy the trail!</font></p>

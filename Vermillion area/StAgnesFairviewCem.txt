<p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; color: #0468e1;">Just a simple hide in the corner of a little Clay County cemetery.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; color: #023a7e;">If mourners are present, or a service is happening, please come back another time.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; color: #0468e1;">The cache is placed out of the way from any gravestones, though you will need to walk through the cemetery to retrieve it. You're looking for an ammo can. The ground can be pretty sloppy in wet conditions (hence the wading attribute), so make sure your footwear is appropriate!</p>

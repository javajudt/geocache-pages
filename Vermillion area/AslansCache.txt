<p style="text-align:center;font-size:large;">I found this guy when he was about 3 months old. I had just picked up my pizza, and he was sitting out in the parking lot. I slowly walked over to him, and he came up to me wanting attention!</p>
<p style="text-align:center;font-size:large;">One day, I had some cache supplies on the floor, and he started playing with the containers. This is the container he seemed to be playing with the most! I found it stashed under the couch a few days later. I wanted to place it over by the parking lot I found him in, but there weren't any good hiding places. This will have to do!</p>
<p style="text-align:center;font-size:large;">The cache isn't a skirt lifter, but it's similar. Please replace it as you found it to avoid it being seen by muggles! Just don't push it up too high.</p>

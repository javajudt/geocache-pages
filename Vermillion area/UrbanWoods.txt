<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #0468e1;">This cache is located along Vermillion's walking/biking path that runs parallel to Stanford Street and Highway 50. The whole trail is extra-wide and paved.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #023a7e;">Please do not park on the road. Park at a business or a side street, and walk to the cache.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #0468e1;">Enjoy a small wooded area sandwiched between traffic and industry! This is a small container, enough room for small trackables and trade items. Although, I did forget to put some swag in it. I may be back to do that, unless you beat me to it!</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #023a7e;">Enjoy the find, and enjoy the trail!</p>

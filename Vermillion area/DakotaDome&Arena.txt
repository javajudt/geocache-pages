<p style="text-align: center; font-size: 24px; color: #ff0000;">DakotaDome and Sanford Arena</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #8b0000;">Since its opening in 1979, the DakotaDome has housed the USD football, swimming, track, volleyball, and basketball teams. The facility can seat 10,000.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #8b0000;">The 118,000-square-foot Sanford Coyote Sports Center was completed in 2016. It will house basketball and volleyball competitions, and can hold 6,000 spectators.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #008000;">This area will definitely be busy during gamedays, so search stealthily! It shouldn't be a hard find after a short search.</p>

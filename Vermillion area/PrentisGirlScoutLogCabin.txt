<p style="text-align: center; font-size: 24px; color: #008000;">Girl Scout Cabin at Prentis Park</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large;">The following information is a quotation from the National Register of Historic Places registration form for Prentis Park. The application was approved in 2001.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; color: #808080;">"In 1933, the log cabin was constructed as a Girl Scout hut and as a memorial by the '76 Club. Over the years it was neglected and suffered from minor vandalism. In 2000, the city gave the Girl Scouts a grant to repair windows and replace the door. Near the Girl Scout Cabin is a historic Horseshoe Court. This facility was added to the park in the 1940's."</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #0000ff;">This cache is definitely one of my favorites out of what I have placed. I got the idea for it from one we found in Seattle during the 2015 Geocaching Block Party. Absolutely no tools are necessary to retrieve the cache...so don't use any! Be sure to use stealth while hunting, especially during nicer weather, because many people enjoy hanging out around the park!</p>

In an effort to get more use out of the Cowboy Trail, I'm taking on BBM&J's challenge of placing a cache at least once every mile on the Cowboy Trail from Norfolk to Valentine. I also encourage YOU to also take on this challenge!

This is located 11 miles "as the crow flies" from the Trailhead in Norfolk.

The trail can be busy with muggles if the weather is nice, or it can be dead in super hot or cold or rainy or snowy weather. Exercise stealth as needed. You're looking for a small, uncamo'd container.

Signal 98

Best music this side of the lily pad! Only on 98.3 KGHQ.

Welcome to this 2-stage multi cache! The posted coordinates (first stage) will take you to a residential street. There is no physical container here (for this cache). But that's okay! You don't need to leave your vehicle for this stage. In fact, you shouldn't leave your vehicle for this stage! There is room to park on 14th Street, south of Park Avenue. Please don't pull into any driveways.

There is no physical container at the posted coordinates; however, you do need to visit the area of the posted coordinates. Use the clues on this page to figure out how to solve for the final location (second stage). A special TOTT is required to solve the puzzle. The final container will be an easy find. There may be room for a couple small trackables or trade items.

When you get the coordinates, put each number in this format:
N ##° 0#.###
W ##° ##.###

Update April 7, 2019: Once you have the final coordinates, please do the following:
To the north coordinate, subtract 0.055.
To the west coordinate, add 0.041.

The best music lives at 98.3 - Signal 98! Enjoy the hunt!

Nebraska Spirit Quest

"Spirit Quest" caches are popular in a number of states. Simply put, a Spirit Quest cache is a cache that is hidden in a cemetery. Some states have a group of SQ cache hiders, and for others it's more informal. There's only a handful of caches in Nebraska named with "NESQ", "SQ", or "Nebraska Spirit Quest". I'd like to see this naming convention catch on so that it's easier to identify cemetery caches.

St. Patrick's Cemetery is located on the northeast edge of Battle Creek, off of McAllister Street. I couldn't find a lot of info about this Catholic cemetery, but the earliest death dates date back to the 1870s. It is still in use today and well-maintained.

You're looking for a small container. It is located inside the cemetery, but away from the graves. Please respect mourners or services when present. Enjoy!

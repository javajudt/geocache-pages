Welcome to Sherwood Forest Park in Woodland Park! The park is open until 10:30 pm. RV pads with electricity are available, as well as picnic shelters, paved trails, and a playground for the kiddos.

This cache is located in a relatively new section of the park. A paved trail connects the two sections across a wetland area. There is also a separate parking area here if you would rather drive.

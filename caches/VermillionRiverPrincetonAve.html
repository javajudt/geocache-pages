Here's a regular size cache for your hunting pleasure. Approach from the south, unless you're wanting to go for a swim. I would not recommend attempting this at night. You do get fairly close to the river, so watch your step! Remember, geocaching can be dangerous--then again, so can anything you do outside. It may be wise to wear long pants and junker shoes. There's a little bit of a walk after you park. Enjoy!

The 3rd Annual Northeast Nebraska Holiday Cache Bash is where it all started. During the Chinese gift exchange, Mr. B of BBM&J picked a nice small gift which happened to be 12 bison tubes. Not knowing where those would end up, 8601dephinium stole them when it was his turn to select a gift in order to prevent them from being placed around Norfolk. That is where this idea was born: "12 Lumps O' Coal for BBM&J." This is all in fun and team BBM&J will get a certificate if they can find them all!

12 Lumps O' Coal for BBM&J #5 - Purple Prancer...because everyone knows Prancer is purple*!

Each new number in the series will be further from Norfolk than the previous. In total, 12 caches will be published. Below is a link to all the caches in this little mini series of fun! Each cache will be a bison tube from the gift stolen from Mr. B! To make this even more fun, they will be hidden by various cachers in the state. Look forward to more fun with this series.

Cache contains a log only, so BYOP. Please use stealth! No need to cross any fences.

 

*No, kids, Prancer isn't really purple.

Warnerville is a small, unincorporated community located a few miles south-southeast of Norfolk. They had their own post office from 1887-1917. One of the features of this area, which you can see from this cache, is Warnerville School. It was established in 1873 and closed around 2008 or 2009, after a push by the state to consolidate smaller schools. The school building holds several classrooms, a gym, two kitches, and a bathroom. After closing, the building and land were auctioned off, and a local family bought it as their new home. Here they raise chickens and grow wheat and produce.

This is a simple micro cache located near Warnerville. Enjoy the area and the cache!

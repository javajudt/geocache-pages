*TO SOLVE:*

The starting coordinates will take you to a group of signs. The one on top has 3 large numbers. Refer to them as ABC. The bottom one has a phone number. Refer to those as D-EFG-HIJ-KLMN. Plug in the numbers to get the final coords!

N A(D+D)° FI.CK(G+C)
W LBJ° (K-J)J.FD(M/2)

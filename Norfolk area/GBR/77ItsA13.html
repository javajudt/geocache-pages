*~Not at the Posted Coordinates!!~*

I got word that there aren't very many puzzle/unknown caches in the US with a difficulty/terrain of 1/3....so I hid one! [:D] It's a really simple puzzle (hence the 1 difficulty). Solve it below!

To the posted north coordinates, add -00.003.
To the posted west coordinates, add -00.816

You're looking for a homemade container. Stay safe and keep on cachin'!

 

GENERAL REMINDERS: Do not cross fences, replace cache to original location, replace camo as best as possible, and don't hurt yourself! You are caching at your own risk!

**This cache was made available, prior to publication, to attendees of the Norfolk Area Fall Caching Extravaganza 2012**

This was placed on leap day. February 29, 2012. Only happens once every 4 years. I thought this should be an 8-day week with 3 days of weekend. I was asked what the extra day would be called, so I looked up the Latin word/phrase for "Leap Day" and it said "Exilient Die". I combined it to make "Exilientdieday", so that's where this cache's name comes from.

You're looking for a seed cache out of "FEB 2ND" (GC3C09Z).

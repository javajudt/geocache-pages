Nebraska Spirit Quest

"Spirit Quest" caches are popular in a number of states. Simply put, a Spirit Quest cache is a cache that is hidden in a cemetery. Some states have a group of SQ cache hiders, and for others it's more informal. At the time of this writing, I found 4 caches in Nebraska with "Nebraska Spirit Quest" or "SQ" in the name. I'd like to see this naming convention catch on so that it's easier to identify cemetery caches.

Located at 550th Ave and 838th Rd (S Airport Rd), Allbery Cemetery is a small cemetery located a few miles southwest of Norfolk. The cemetery has been active for more than 127 years, and there are headstones dating back to the 1880s. It once belonged to the Methodist Church, but ownership was transfered to Madison County in 1976. This is where the county buries those who can't afford to bury themselves. There is a variety of grave markers here, including a very unique one you'll probably see on the way to the cache.

You're looking for a small container. It is located inside the cemetery, but not too close to any graves. Please respect mourners or services when present. Enjoy!

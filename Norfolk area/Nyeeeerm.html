This is a simple roadside cache within view of the Norfolk Regional Airport, which sits to the east. Maybe you'll see a plane come in or take off! Be sure to pull to the side of the road. It doesn't seem like too busy of a road, but that could depend on the time of day. You're looking for a regular size cache!

To encourage more local cache placement, I've included 3 ready-to-go micro cache containers for someone to take and hide somewhere! No need to trade for these, but please only take 1 per caching name. Remember that it's recommended to find at least 20 caches before placing one.

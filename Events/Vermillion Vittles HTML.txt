<p style="color:darkblue;">Quick Info:</p>
<p style="color:cornflowerblue;">What: Vermillion Vittles, a monthly meet-and-greet<br />
When: Saturday, April 21st, 11am-12pm (approximate times)<br />
Where: Mister Smith's Cafe & Bakery</p>


<hr />
<p> </p>
<p style="text-align:center;">Join us on <strong>Saturday, April 21st,</strong> for</p>
<h1 style="text-align:center;color:navy;"><strong>Vermillion Vittles</strong></h1>
<p> </p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:green;">I am graduating from college on May 5th! As of right now, I also have no idea where I'll end up after May 5th. Sooo this may very well be the last Vermillion Vittles (at least the last one hosted by 'PuterBoy)!</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:darkgreen;">For April's event, we will meet at Mister Smith's around 11 and chit chat until 12...or however long everyone ends up staying! If you've never been here, Mister Smith's is located inside of Ace Hardware. Don't be confused when you get to the posted coordinates. [:)] As always, no purchase is necessary to sit and talk.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:green;">All you need to bring is a mouth to talk and ears to listen. (Okay, you're welcome even if you forget these at home.) We welcome all kinds of people, but caching people seem to visit the most!<br />
If you're new to the cacher-community: stop on by, don't be shy! We can answer any questions you may have.<br />
If you're a veteran: I need not say another word.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:orangered;">What do you say? Sound like fun? Great! See you <strong>Saturday</strong>!</p>

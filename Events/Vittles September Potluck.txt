<p style="color:darkblue;">Quick Info:</p>
<p style="color:cornflowerblue;">What: Vermillion Vittles, a monthly meet-and-greet<br />
When: Saturday, September 23rd, 12pm-1pm (approximate times)<br />
Where: Prentis Park</p>


<hr />
<p> </p>
<p style="text-align:center;">Join us on <strong>Saturday, September 23rd,</strong> for</p>
<h1 style="text-align:center;color:navy;"><strong>Vermillion Vittles</strong></h1>
<p> </p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:darkgreen;">Since last year's event was so successful, how about another potluck picnic? August's schedule didn't allow for an event, so we'll try to make September work. We will meet at the north shelter in Prentis Park around 12 and stay until at least 1.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:blue;">In case you don't know how a potluck-style event works, each person or group who wants to eat should bring a food item to share with everyone else in attendence. For your thirst, it will be easiest to BYOB. Pro tip: post with your Will Attend what you plan on bringing so we don't end up with 20 bags of chips! We'll have the grill going, so grillable things are welcome. I'll bring hot dogs.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:green;">We welcome all kinds of people, from new cachers to veteran cachers--even muggles who want to learn! If you're new to the cacher-community: stop on by, don't be shy! We can answer any questions you may have.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:purple;">I put in an order for nice weather, but they said it's not guaranteed for late September in South Dakota. In case it rains, snows, or does anything else unfavorable, we can change plans. I'll watch the forecast and send out announcements.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:orangered;">What do you say? Sound like fun? Great! See you <strong>Saturday</strong>!</p>

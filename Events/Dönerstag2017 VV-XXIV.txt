Where: Dairy Queen</p>

<p style="text-align:center;">According to the <a target="_blank" href="https://www.geocaching.com/blog/2017/02/where-in-the-world-is-signal-hint-gruse-aus-deutschland/">Geocaching Blog</a>, "Dönerstag began in 2007 when German cachers met a kebab shops around the country all at the same time." Dönerstag is a pun on the German words <i>Donnerstag</i> (Thursday) and <i>Döner</i> (Kebab). This year Geocaching HQ is awarding souvenirs to those who attend events on the 11th annual Dönerstag!</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:red;">Come celebrate Dönerstag 2017 in one of Ratingen, Germany's eight partner cities! Unfortunately, Vermillion does not have any kebab shops, so Dairy Queen will have to do. We'll meet around 6:30 pm until 7:30ish pm. No purchase is necessary to join.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:gold;">What do you say? Sound like fun? Great! See you <strong>Thursday</strong>!</p>

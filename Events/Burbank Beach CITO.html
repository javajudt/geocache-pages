Cotton Park CITO

 

Cotton Park is located below the bluff in Vermillion. It features a playground, a dog park, and a paved trail that runs along the Vermillion River.

It's CITO week, and we need YOUR help! Come make your mark by cleaning up the park. We'll meet around 9:30 am and plan to stay about an hour and a half, depending on litter density. Trash bags will be provided. If you wish to use gloves or a picker-upper tool, be sure to bring them. Fingers crossed that the weather will be decent!

The terrain is pretty even for the most part; but if you decide you physically can't participate, you're still more than welcome to attend by handing out trash bags in the parking lot and directing people where to go.

I can't wait to see your smiling, helpful faces on Saturday!

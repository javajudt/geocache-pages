Quick Info:

What: Nobody Likes You When You're 23
When: Saturday, March 9, 11:30 AM - 1 PM (approximate times)
Where: Pizza Ranch

 

What's my age again? According to the band Blink-182, nobody likes you when you're 23. That said, let's meet for lunch on Saturday the 9th--just 3 days before people stop liking me! [:P]

We'll gather, eat, share stories, and everything else from 11:30 until 1:00 (or until everybody leaves). Pizza Ranch offers a variety of buffet items, but as always, no purchase is necessary to join us.

Full disclosusre: For those who have attended "p.boy's Birthday Bonanza" in the past, this event probably won't be quite as extravagant. This is more of an excuse to get together and share stories, and for folks to come do some caching in the Norfolk area if the weather cooperates. Although I won't say there's no possibility of a few new caches in the area! [;)]

What do you say? Sound like fun? Great! See you Saturday!

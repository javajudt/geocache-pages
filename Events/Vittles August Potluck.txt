<p style="text-align:center;">Join us on <strong>International Geocaching Day - Saturday, August 20th</strong> - for</p>

<h1 style="text-align:center;color:navy;"><strong>Vermillion Vittles</strong></h1>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p style="text-align:center;">Two things: 1) I messed up and forgot all about July&#39;s event, and 2) today is International Geocaching Day! How about a potluck picnic to celebrate today&#39;s festivities? Let&#39;s&nbsp;meet at the north&nbsp;shelter in Prentis Park&nbsp;around 11. We&#39;ll stay until at least 12.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:blue;">In case you don&#39;t know how a potluck-style event works, each person or group who wants to eat&nbsp;should bring a food item to share with everyone else in attendence. For your thirst, it will be easiest to BYOB.&nbsp;Pro tip: post with your Will Attend&nbsp;what you plan on bringing so we don&#39;t end up with 20 bags of chips! We&#39;ll have the grill going, so grillable things are welcome. I&#39;ll bring hot dogs.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:darkorange;">I&#39;m planning&nbsp;to place several new caches before this day, so you&nbsp;certainly shouldn&#39;t&nbsp;be bored! [:)]</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:green;">We welcome all kinds of people, from new cachers to veteran cachers--even muggles who want to learn!&nbsp;If you&#39;re new to the cacher-community: stop on by, don&#39;t be shy!&nbsp;We can answer any questions you may have.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:mediumpurple;">Oh! One more thing. I put in an order for nice weather;&nbsp;but in case it rains, snows, or does anything else unfavorable, we can change plans and meet at Burger King. I&#39;ll watch the forecast and send out announcements.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;color:orangered;">What do you say? Sound like fun? Great! See you <strong>Saturday</strong>!</p>

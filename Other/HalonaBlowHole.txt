<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #008000;"><em>Aloha from Nebraska!</em></p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #0468e1;">While vacationing in beautiful Hawai'i, GeoJudt and I thought it would be fun to place a couple caches.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #023a7e;">This cache will take you to a shrine near Halona Blow Hole. The blow hole is an amazing spectacle of nature. Just go to the observation point to watch.</p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: large; color: #0468e1;">Please park in the pulloff area. Shouldn't be too hard of a find for the veteran cacher. Hopefully not for newbies, either, but you never know.</p>

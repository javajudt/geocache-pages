This cache is placed in the smaller of Woodland Park's parks. For a while the park almost seemed abandoned, but it appears to be maintained today. This park includes a playground and a basketless disc golf course. (At least that's my best guess for the scattered posts in the ground with numbers on them!) The parking area for the park is only accessible from Meadow Lane. The park closes at 10:30 pm.

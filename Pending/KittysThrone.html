I am shamelessly borrowing the idea for this cache from an online post I saw a while back for a similar cache in the Sioux Falls area.

This cache is located in the front yard of a private residence. The house is the second house south of Park Avenue. Permission has been granted for placement. Please don't park in the driveway, and please no caching between 10pm-7am.

The cache itself should be easy to find, but there's an extra twist to get to the log. All tools you need are provided with the cache. You must sign your name to the official log in order to log this cache online. Please no swag or trackables in this cache.
